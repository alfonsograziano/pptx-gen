# Title cover

A cover slide: an overline, a large title, and a one-line subtitle on a dark background.

## Layout
- Dark (`ink`) full-bleed background.
- Small accent overline near the top.
- Large title filling the upper-middle.
- One-line subtitle below, in a muted tone.
- No header underscore and no footer: this is a cover.

## When to use
- The opening slide of a deck.
- A section cover where you want a bold, quiet title moment.

## When not to use
- Slides that carry real content: use `content-lead-bullets` or your own template.

## Fields
- `overline-label` — short kicker above the title.
- `your-presentation-title-goes-here` — the main title.
- `a-short-subtitle-that-sets-up-the-st` — the one-line subtitle.
